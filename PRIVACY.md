# Privacy Policy

**Meridian** is a free, open-source Windows desktop application for managing your calendars and tasks across multiple providers (Google, Microsoft Outlook, and others).

## Data Collection

Meridian does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

## Calendar and Task Provider Access

Meridian uses OAuth 2.0 to access your calendar and task data from supported providers. This access is used to read and manage your events and tasks within the application on your local device.

- Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device only (e.g. `%APPDATA%\Meridian\tokens\`) and are used only to communicate with the respective provider's APIs on your behalf. Your credentials are never transmitted to or stored on any remote server.
- No calendar or task data leaves your device except as part of direct API requests to the respective provider's servers.

## Third-Party Services

Meridian communicates only with the APIs of calendar and task providers you explicitly connect (such as Google APIs or Microsoft Graph API). No other third-party services receive your data.

## Open Source

Meridian is fully open source. You can review all source code at https://github.com/Phoenix-/meridian.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/Phoenix-/meridian/issues.
